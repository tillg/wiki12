---
name: autonomous
description: Working alone, w/o having the user for feedback or answering questions. Use when user wants to make progress on a plan but doesn't have the time or energy or is not available.
---

<what-to-do>
Make progress on the plan, even if you have to make assumptions and decisions on your own. If you reach a point where you need user input, make a decision based on your best judgement and move forward. Don't wait for user feedback to keep making progress. Keep track of all the decisions you make and assumptions you have to make in a .md file, so that you can review them with the user at the end. When you are done, present the .md file with the decisions.
</what-to-do>
